About Dr. Karim

Compassionate Care

A dedicated Surgeon-Scientist combining clinical excellence with cutting-edge research

Dr. Karim SERHAN is an accomplished Consultant Surgeon with over 14 years of experience in delivering exceptional surgical care across three countries: Lebanon, USA, and UAE.

He is recognized by colleagues for his commitment to patients, compassionate care, superior surgical skills, and innovative research mindset.

Dr. Karim is a Diplomate of the American Board of Surgery, an Associate Fellow of the American College of Surgeons, and an Adjunct Assistant Professor at the United Arab Emirates University.
